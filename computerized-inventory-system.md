TL;DR: A computerized inventory system replaces manual stock tracking with real-time, digital control, helping Indian SME manufacturers reduce errors, plan better, and respond faster to demand changes.

Computerized inventory systems play a critical role in modern manufacturing, where accuracy, speed, and visibility directly affect profitability.

## What Is a Computerized Inventory System?
A computerized inventory system is a software-based system that tracks inventory digitally and updates stock levels in real time.

## FAQs on Computerized Inventory Systems

### What is a computerized inventory system?
It is a digital system that tracks and manages inventory automatically.
