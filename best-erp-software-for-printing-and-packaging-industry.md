The printing and packaging industry manufactures products like newspapers, catalogs, calendars, plastic and glass containers, etc. These products are usually required in bulk by the customers. While manufacturing, companies need to follow complicated production processes. ERP for printing and packaging industry helps businesses automate these processes and improve operational efficiency. 

In this blog post, we will learn the importance of ERP for printing and packaging industry and how it can help you increase business efficiency.

## Understanding ERP For Printing Industry
ERP for printing industry is useful for improving production processes and order management. It simplifies managing complex tasks like creating digital files for printing, plate making, printing, finishing, quality control, and shipping. ERP system for printing industry also improves inventory management through barcoding and batch tracking features. 

A printing ERP software allows businesses to have controlled levels of raw materials, whether different types of inks, plates, films, or binding materials.

You can also set reorder points in ERP software for printing industry to improve your procurement.  It also helps you create and manage quotations, purchase requisitions, and invoices. ERP solution overall improves your decision-making process and production planning features.

## Understanding ERP For Packaging Industry
ERP for packaging industry is a set of tools that help automate processes, speed up operations, reduce errors, and increase operational efficiency. ERP software for packaging industry offers features like batch-tracking to manage all types of raw materials, whether plastics, glasses, metals, inks, coatings, or adhesives.

ERP software for packaging businesses improves the production cycle from enquiry to dipatch, including quality control, and distribution. Packaging manufacturing software suggests the right materials requirements and in accurate quantities to achieve your production targets. The accounting features in the packaging industry ERP help you with accurate product costing, sales and purchase register, and compliance with GST.

## The Need Of ERP System For Printing And Packaging Industry
The printing and packaging industry needs to follow several steps on a daily basis, such as designing, material selection, printing, decoration, cutting, shaping, assembly, and distribution to fulfill orders on time. All these steps require high accuracy; otherwise, a lot of waste can occur. Even small mistakes in products like food packages and advertising banners can lead to upset customers and reduce the brand’s credibility in the market.

ERP for printing and packaging industry helps overcome these challenges and increase operational efficiency by keeping transparency throughout the process. It provides real-time data and help you monitor inventory levels, production processes, product quality, machines & workers’ performances, and more. 

ERP for printing and packaging industry allows you to utilize different techniques and methods to manage your inventory, such as [FIFO](/blogs/fifo-method), [LIFO](/blogs/lifo-method), [Just-in-Time](/blogs/just-in-time), and [ABC analysis](/blogs/abc-analysis-in-inventory-management). With ERP software for printing industry, you can also choose different production methods, such as make-to-order, make-to-stock, and batch-manufacturing, to improve your demand management.

## Key Features To Look For In ERP Software For Printing And Packaging Industry
Let’s learn about the key features of printing and packaging ERP software that can be useful for your company.
* **Job Estimation:** It allows you to analyze jobs or orders so that you can predict time, workforce, and material requirements and improve your planning and [order fulfilment](/blogs/order-fulfillment).
* **Artwork Approval:** You need to get approvals from a lot of authorities for your artwork and designs of printing and packaging products. ERP for printing and packaging industry allows you to automate this process and save a lot of time.
* **Inventory Management:** It allows you to manage your raw materials and [finished goods](/blogs/finished-goods) in many ways, like batched inventory and categorized items. It offers multiple units of measurement and barcode generation features to manage your inventory smoothly.
* **Work-In-Progress Monitoring:** This feature allows you to keep an eye on your workforce, machines, and operations. It helps you reduce waste and downtime and complete work on time to manage your delivery dates.
* **Bills Of Materials Management:** ERP for printing and packaging industry helps you calculate the costs involved in each process of making printing and packaging products. It analyzes each process, whether plate making, printing, cutting, or decoration and calculates the right costs, including labour charges, electricity bills, material expenses, etc.
* **Quality Control:** It is an important feature that helps you make sure that you are meeting industry standards when manufacturing your printing and packaging products.
* **Customer Relationship Management:** It helps you interact with your customers and improve your lead management. It allows you to analyze customer preferences, make informed decisions, and expand your business.
