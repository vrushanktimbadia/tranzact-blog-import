### Understanding Digital Documentation
Digital documentation is the process of converting hard-copy files into electronic documents. This process includes the integration of tools and technologies to speed up the work with accuracy. Digital documents can be electronically stored in computers or [cloud ERP software](/blogs/cloud-erp-software) and can be accessed easily anytime and anywhere you want.

Digital-based documents can facilitate collaboration through easy file sharing within the organization and suppliers. They also help you automate documentation for GST compliance and data analytics to make more informed decisions.

### Common Challenges Faced During The Transition From Paper To Digital Documentation
You may face some challenges when making this transition. Knowing it early can simplify your digital transformation with effective preparation.

* **Time-consuming:** It may take months or even years, especially if you have a large volume of documents to work on.
* **Resource Unavailability:** Lack of resources like scanning equipment and storage solutions can cause problems in digitizing paper documents.
* **Integration Issues:** If you are using traditional ERP software, you may face problems integrating your document management system with it.
* **Equipment Breakdown:** The software and equipment you are using might cause malfunction due to overloaded work.
* **Expensive Training:** It may cost you more to train your employees to organize files and maintain the data.
* **Data Loss:** During the digitization process, there is a risk of losing important data due to improper handling, technical failures, or inadequate backup systems.

### Strategies To Address And Overcome These Challenges
Digital transformation of your paper documents can be time-consuming and even cause equipment failure. Here are the right strategies to follow and overcome the challenges while digitizing paper documents.

* **Preparing Ahead:** Start by choosing important or high-volume files to convert paper to digital documents. Once it is done, you can continue with the remaining files.
* **Choosing Right Tools:** Use equipment like a bar code scanner, RFID, unlimited cloud storage, and cloud-ERP software to easily manage and maintain your documentation process.
* **Integration Capabilities:** Look for specifications of [digital transformation in manufacturing](/blogs/digital-transformation-in-manufacturing) systems that match your existing system and help you with data scalability.
* **High-Quality Equipment:** Invest in scanners, storage devices, document management software, and more that can run for a long time.
* **Change Management:** Train your employees about the new tools and technologies and communicate to support them in adapting to the change in digital documentation.
* **Security Measures:** Consider integrating cloud-based software for data management with features like encryptions, access control, and regular security audits.

## Step-By-Step Guide To Digitizing Paper Documents
Here is a step-by-step guide to effectively transform your paper files into digital format with ease.

### Step 1: Sorting And Categorizing Paper Documents
Start organizing your important files like your business reports, duplicate files, and client information. Audit and shortlist the files that are valuable for your business because the more documents you want to digitise, the more you have to pay. You can incorporate ILM (Information Lifecycle management) that will help establish a retention schedule for converting paper documents to electronic files while following industry compliance.

### Step 2: Choosing The Right Equipment And Software
There are many ways used for converting paper to electronic documents, and scanners are among the best options. Select scanners that can scan large volumes of files without compromising quality and resolution. You can choose TranZact for document management and storage, as it provides a cloud system to store and manage your important files automatically.

### Step 3: Scanning And Converting Paper Documents To Digital Format
Paper documents can be digitized through the combination of an effective scanner and digital documentation tools. You can choose a high-quality scanner to scan a large volume of files while categorizing them according to their values, name, and number. You can consider OCR (Optical Character Recognition) software to convert them into machine language and then have easy access through ERP software.

### Step 4: Organizing And Storing Digital Documents
Make sure to organize your files based on type, name, importance, and other factors while digitizing paper documents. You can start creating an electronic file structure that contains folders with file names similar to those of similar cabinets. You can choose a secured cloud-based storage space to keep your files safe and easily accessible by your team whenever required. For example, if your finance department has a cabinet labelled "Invoices," create a corresponding folder in your digital storage with the same name.

### Step 5: Ensuring Data Security And Compliance
It is always a good practice to protect your digital documents from theft, cyberattacks, and other vulnerabilities. Choose the cloud-ERP software that provides security measures to protect it from getting hacked while following all compliance with industry standards. Look for features like encryption, access control, secured digital sharing, regular data backups, and additional security.

## How To Convert Paper Documents Into Digital Format?
Converting paper documents to digital files can be time-consuming and challenging for SME manufacturers. It requires high-quality tools and document management software to convert paper into digital files. Here is how to convert paper documents into digital format with ease using different methods.

* **Complete Backlog:** It is the process of digitalizing paper files into electronic documents through IoT devices like scanners, [barcode scanning system](/blogs/barcode-technology), and more. You can easily store them in the form of images and access them through your computer or smartphone.
* **Image On-Demand:** This process is usually executed when document scanning is not possible practically. It involves a partnership with document management vendors where you need to request document arrangement, repair for damaged files, and scanning to convert paper to digital documents.
* **Day-Forward Digitization:** This is a business process methodology service where a vendor helps to reduce the volume of paper transactions. In this process, the paper that comes first is scanned away, organized, and then stored for easy access.
